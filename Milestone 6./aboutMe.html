About Jeremy:

Born and raised in the heart of Northern California, Jeremy learned early on to appreciate the opportunities given and take nothing for granted. In 2012, Jeremy moved to Las Vegas Nevada and began his career in technology at Sony Playstation. Jeremy has a passion for technology and video gaming, working tech support for some of the largest companies in the world such as Verizon Wireless. In 2020 Jeremy opened a Pokemon fan group called JermzGenz and used his platform to begin learning about content creation, distribution, and marketing. In 2021 Jeremy embarked on new journeys, finding a career in Vertical SaaS(Software as a Service), and beginning his education in Software Development. Over the years Jeremy has learned how to create clean, functional, and maintainable code in several languages and seeks to further his career in a
Software Development role.
